## Why OrchStep?

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="No platform required" icon="laptop">
		A single CLI binary. Runs inside your existing CI/CD — no SaaS signup, no agents to install.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Shell-first" icon="seti:shell">
		Delegates to your existing tools: terraform, kubectl, aws, docker. OrchStep orchestrates; your tools execute.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Built for AI agents" icon="rocket">
		MCP server, skill documents, and `--format json` on every command. First-class LLM integration.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Module ecosystem" icon="puzzle">
		Reusable components for common tasks. Publish to the registry or keep them private.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>
